We work tirelessly with you from planning and inception all the way through completion. No hidden costs or surprises.

Our Services

Site Survey

All projects start with a site survey. Zoning by-laws, site orientation and right of ways are vital items we look for at this stage.

Architecture / Home Design

We work with both Architects and Home Designers to plan the structure, room layouts, floor plans and more. Approved plans are then sent for building permits.

Contracts & Permits

After you make your selections and customize your home, we will take care of the rest by securing the necessary permits, contracts and paperwork needed to begin construction on your new home.

Design & Materials

We can work with your interior designer or use ours to select the materials that fit both your taste and budget.

Walk Throughs & Tours

Our team will organize a personal framing and electrical tour to review the building at various stages of construction.

Cabinets & Mill Work

We work with the best mill workers and cabinet makers that can take your designs and transform them to quality cabinets.

Quality Inspection

Before you move-in, you will have a quality assurance walk-through with an SVM team member. This includes marking any deficiencies to be corrected promptly.

Support

Our work does not stop after you move in. We are there to support you after as well.
