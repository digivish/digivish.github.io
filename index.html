Our Process

  • Consultation
  • Home Design
  • Design & Materials
  • Home Build
  • Quality Inspection
  • Support
All projects start with an initial consultation where we understand your ideas, requirements and your lot details. A site survey helps us provide guidance on zoning by-laws, site orientation, building lines and general feasibility. At this stage, we also start putting together an initial budget for you to approve.
We work with both architects and home designers to plan the building structure, room layouts, space and floor plans, outdoor landscape and more. Approved designs are then sent for appropriate building permits.
We can work with your own interior designer or you could use our in-house designer to select interior materials, colour, fit and finish that works with your taste and budget.
We start building after the appropriate permits have been approved and secured. These activities including demolition (if required), foundation, exterior framing, roofing, flooring, mill-work and every detail designed and conceived to completely build the house of your dreams.
Although quality is inscpedted by the city at every stage of the build, we strive to go further. We facilitate personal framing, electrical, plumbing and flooring tours to review our building at various stages of construction. Before you move-in, we go through a quality assurance walk-through with you to identify any defects, flaws or inconsistencies that we promptly fix.
Our work does not stop with your move-in. We are available to provide support as necessary and as advised with our 2-5-10 New Home Warranty.
